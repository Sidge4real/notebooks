# Bachelor Level Notes

These notes are written specifically for the **Professional Bachelor – Computer Networks** course.  
They focus on **conceptual understanding**, **practical configuration**, and **troubleshooting skills**, aligned with the expectations of the UCLL Studie‑GPS and the course lecturer.

This folder intentionally avoids deep‑dive, protocol‑level details.  
My original notes (in `/notes`) follow a network‑engineer trajectory and contain CCNA+/CCNP‑level depth.  
Those remain valuable for future specialization, but they exceed the required level for this course.
