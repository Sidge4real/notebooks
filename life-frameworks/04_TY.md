## What are the TY Rules?

TY = **Thank You (others) / Thank Yourself (me)**.

The TY Rules are a set of principles that guide your interactions, mindset and growth.  
They remind you to:

- thank yourself for the work you’ve done  
- thank others for the role they played  
- stay grounded in humility  
- stay consistent in discipline  
- stay aligned with your identity  

Gratitude is not softness — it is **clarity**, **ownership**, and **maturity**.

Be humble!

---

## TY Rules

### 1. TY for the effort  
Thank yourself for showing up, even when it was difficult.  
Growth begins with acknowledging your own discipline.

### 2. TY for the failure  
Thank yourself for the mistakes — they are data, not identity.  
Failure is part of capability building.

### 3. TY for the lesson  
Every experience teaches you something.  
Capture the lesson before you move on.

### 4. TY for the boundaries  
Thank yourself for saying no.  
Boundaries protect your identity and your energy.

### 5. TY for the discipline  
Consistency is a gift you give to your future self.

### 6. TY for the people who helped  
No one grows alone.  
Thank the people who contributed to your journey — directly or indirectly.

### 7. TY for the people who challenged you  
Critics, doubters, and obstacles sharpen your maturity.

### 8. TY for the environment you build  
Your systems, habits and surroundings shape who you become.

### 9. TY for the identity you are creating  
Every action reinforces or weakens your identity.  
Thank yourself for choosing the aligned path.

### 10. TY for the journey  
Growth is not a destination — it is a continuous loop of becoming.
