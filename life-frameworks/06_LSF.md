## What is the Life Systems Framework?
The Life Systems Framework is a comprehensive system for your life and work.  
It helps you design your environment, your routines, your relationships, and your mindset for success.

## Health System
- Don't over-eat, but also don't under-eat.
- Don't over-drink, but also don't under-drink.
- Don't over-sleep, but also don't under-sleep.
- Don't over-exercise, but also don't under-exercise.
- Don't over-relax, but also don't under-relax.
- Don't over-stimulate, but also don't under-stimulate.
- Don't over-medicate, but also don't under-medicate.

Build systems around those principles which push a healthy lifestyle, but also allow for flexibility and enjoyment.

## Work System
- Don't overwork, but also don't underwork. Find the right balance for you.
- Focus on the work that matters, not just the work that is urgent.
- Build a work system that supports your identity and values.
- Build a work system that supports your goals and aspirations.
- Build a work system that supports your energy and vibe.
- Build a work system that supports your growth and development.
- Don't work underpaid, but also don't work overpaid. Find the right balance for you.
- Don't work in a toxic environment, but also don't work in a too comfortable environment. Find the right balance for you.
- Don't work in a job that doesn't align with your identity and values, but also don't work in a job that is too risky. Find the right balance for you.
- If jobs don't furrfil you, start your own business. If your own business doesn't furrfil you, find a job that does. Find the right balance for you.
- Build wealth, not income. Build a career, not a job. Build a legacy, not a paycheck.

## Relationship System

- Stay true to your identity and values in your relationships.
- Invest in the relationships that matter to you.
- Let go of the relationships that don't matter to you.
- Communicate clearly and honestly in your relationships.
- Set boundaries in your relationships.
- Show appreciation and gratitude in your relationships.
- Be present and attentive in your relationships.
- Be supportive and encouraging in your relationships.

Example:
- I invest time in a relationship when we both are on the same energy and vibe. I let go of a relationship when we are on different energies and vibes. I communicate clearly and honestly in my relationships, and I set boundaries when necessary. I show appreciation and gratitude in my relationships, and I am present and attentive in my relationships. I am supportive and encouraging in my relationships.
- If someone has a total complete other view on life, I will not invest time in that relationship. If someone has a similar view on life, I will invest time in that relationship. If someone has a different view on life, but we are on the same energy and vibe, I will invest time in that relationship. If someone has a different view on life, and we are on different energies and vibes, I will let go of that relationship.
- If I am dating, I stay true to my christian identity and values. I invest in the relationship when we are on the same energy and vibe. I let go of the relationship when we are on different energies and vibes. I communicate clearly and honestly in the relationship, and I set boundaries when necessary. I show appreciation and gratitude in the relationship, and I am present and attentive in the relationship. I am supportive and encouraging in the relationship.
- I quit going for investing in current person I date with if she doesn't want to invest in next step. Or when she had a lot of people who she was with, it's a questionable person to invest in. Etc.

## Time System

**Make time for what matters.**

Preparation for the week/month:
- Mail prepping, 1 day per month cooking for complete month. 
- 1 evening per week reflection & planning per week.
- 1 half day working on content (4 hours).

"complete" week:
- 5 days working on mainstream income (40 hours).
- 1 day working on coaching (8 hours).
- 1 evening sessions per week, investing portfolio.
- 2 evening sessions per week, learning (reading, courses, etc.).
- 2 evenings sending LinkedIn DM's for coaching, networking or new job.
- other hours is taking a breath, go for a walk or gym.

Some weeks:
- More time in content
OR
- More time in learning
OR
- More time in coaching

Example of a week:
- **Monday**: 8 hours work, 1 hour learning.
- **Tuesday**: 8 hours work, 1 hour DM's.
- **Wednesday**: 8 hours work, 1 hour investing.
- **Thursday**: 8 hours work, 1 hour DM's.
- **Friday**: 8 hours work, 1 hour learning.
- **Saturday**: 8 hours coaching, 1 hour investing.
- **Sunday**: 4 hours content, 4 homework (e.g. cooking, cleaning, etc.). and 1 hour learning.
