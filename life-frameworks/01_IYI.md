# IYI Framework 
*How the framework is applied in real life today*

Hi, I am Lukas Van der Spiegel *(author)*.  
I created the IYI Framework as a practical system for building a long‑term professional identity for myself. Free to use, adapt and share, I hope it offers clarity, structure and value in your own journey. 

This document explains how each layer of the framework is applied in real life using concrete, actionable techniques.

Questions? hello@lukasvanderspiegel.be or via LinkedIn.

---

## Identity  
*Who you are seen as.*

Identity is expressed through the roles you choose to embody.  
These roles shape how others perceive you and how you position yourself in the world.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- Software Engineer  
- Coach  
- Entrepreneur  

These roles define the public identity I build and reinforce.

---

## Capability  
*What you can actually do.*

Capability is demonstrated through experience, skill development and execution.  
It is the foundation of all future layers.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- Accumulated experience across engineering, coaching and entrepreneurship  
- Demonstrated ability to deliver results  
- Continuous learning and skill expansion  

Experience becomes the core of my capability layer.

---

## Credibility  
*Proof that your capability is real.*

Credibility is built through visible, verifiable contributions.  
It is the bridge between what you can do and what others believe you can do.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- Open‑source contributions  
- Public repositories  
- Documented work  
- Transparent problem‑solving  

Open source acts as evidence of my competence.

---

## Maturity  
*How you behave, communicate and interact.*

Maturity is shown through your behavior, emotional regulation and the stability of your actions.  
It signals reliability and long‑term consistency.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- LinkedIn recommendations (not as validation, but as **network proof**)  
- A strong, diverse professional network  
- Consistent, stable communication  
- Demonstrated emotional and professional maturity  

My network becomes a signal of my maturity.

---

## Authority  
*Being recognized as someone worth listening to.*

Authority grows when you consistently share knowledge, frameworks and insights.  
It is earned through clarity, value and repetition.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- Posting about my domain expertise  
- Sharing frameworks, insights and mental models  
- Publishing my thinking publicly  
- Teaching through content  

Content becomes the vehicle of my authority.

---

## Visibility  
*Being seen by the right people.*

Visibility amplifies authority and connects it back to identity.  
It ensures that the right audience sees the right version of you.

**Current technique (for me):**  
- Connecting all posts back to one central website  
- Using my website as my identity hub  
- Ensuring all content reinforces the same identity  
- Creating a consistent digital footprint  

Visibility ensures my identity is seen, understood and remembered.
