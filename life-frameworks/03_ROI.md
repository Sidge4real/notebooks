# Cross-Domain ROI Principles

Hi, I’m Lukas Van der Spiegel *(author)*. I designed cross‑domain frameworks to shape my way of thinking, decision‑making and long‑term growth.

These frameworks are free to use, adapt and share. I hope they offer clarity, structure and value in your own journey.

## What do I mean with "frameworks"?

A framework is a structured way of asking better questions before acting, deciding or committing resources.  
This document is not about giving answers, but about **expanding your lens**: seeing more dimensions of ROI than just money.

Use this as:
- a thinking checklist before big decisions  
- a reflection tool after actions  
- a design lens when building systems, products, or habits  

---

## Social ROI

Social ROI looks at the impact of your actions on other people and the social fabric around you.  
It asks: *“If I do this, what happens to the people and relationships connected to me?”*

- What is impact on others?
